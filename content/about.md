## Who Am I?

I'm Luca Pelzer, an aspiring IT System Integration Specialist with a growing passion for artificial intelligence. Currently in my apprenticeship, I'm building hands-on experience with enterprise technologies while diving deep into AI - from building agents to running self-hosted models.

## What Do I Do?

I spend my time exploring the AI landscape - experimenting with language models, building AI agents, and running my own AI infrastructure. I believe in understanding technology from the ground up, which means not just using AI tools, but hosting and building them myself.

### Core Focus Areas

- **AI Agents** - Building and orchestrating intelligent agents for automation and problem-solving
- **Self-Hosted AI** - Running local language models and AI infrastructure on my own hardware
- **AI Development** - Prompt engineering, API integrations, and building AI-powered applications
- **Documentation** - Writing about what I learn to help others on the same journey

## What Do I Believe In?

- **Learning by doing** - The best way to understand AI is to build with it
- **Owning your stack** - Self-hosting AI means full control and deeper understanding
- **Sharing knowledge** - If I struggled with something, others probably will too
- **Building in public** - Documenting the journey, mistakes and all

Behind the scenes, I run my own infrastructure on Proxmox VE - hosting everything from AI workloads to this blog. It's the foundation that lets me experiment freely with new AI technologies.

### Current Stack

- **Hypervisor**: Proxmox VE
